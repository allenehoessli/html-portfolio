The Best Movies According to Allen

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A banker wrongly imprisoned for murder forms a deep bond with a fellow inmate over decades, finding hope and redemption behind bars.

The Godfather (1972)

The aging patriarch of a crime dynasty transfers control of his empire to his reluctant son

The Dark Knight (2008)

Batman faces his most formidable foe yet — the Joker — in a psychological and physical battle for Gotham’s soul.
